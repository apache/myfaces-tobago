<tc:menubar>, <tc:menu>, <tc:menuCommand> and <tc:menuSeparator> are deprecated.

Use the bar facet in a content box with a <tc:buttons> tag instead.
For a dropdownmenus have a look at the 'Dropdown' section on the page.
To add a separator just use <tc:separator/>.

<tc:box label="Content Box">
  <f:facet name="bar">
    <tc:buttons>
      <tc:button label="One" omit="true"/>
      <tc:button label="Two" omit="true"/>
      <tc:button label="Three" omit="true">
        <tc:command label="Four"/>
        <tc:command label="Five"/>
        <tc:separator/>
        <tc:command label="Six"/>
      ...
